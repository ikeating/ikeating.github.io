iKeating

Welcome! My name is Isaiah Keating and my family and I live just south of Grand Rapids, Minnesota in Harris Township. Our home is located on the Wendigo arm of Pokegama Lake. After graduating high school in Grand Rapids, I earned my AAS degree in law enforcement from Hibbing Community and Technical College. I served as a Peace officer for twenty-four years, twenty of which for the City of Canby as Chief of Police. During my employment with the City of Canby, I earned my Associate of Arts degree in Liberal Arts (Phi Theta Kappa) from Minnesota West Community College. I then attended Dakota State University's computer science program. While attending DSU, I completed the Computer Science I & II courses but did not recieve a degree. After moving back to Grand Rapids, I applied for, and was accepted as a member of the Itasca County Sheriff’s Department Volunteer Dive Team in 2021.
