2023 Season: Hat of the Matter

  • Tall Timber Days parade – 08/06/2023
  • Fill the Hill – 08/22/2023
  • Apple Valley – 09/09/2023
  • Bands of America – 09/16/2023 6th place overall
  • Rebel Classic Competition in Champlin Park, MN - 10/07/2023. On Saturday evening, Oct. 7, the band took First Place Overall and won Outstanding Music, Best Visuals, Best Percussion, and Best Colorguard. This is the second clean sweep of Class AA this year for the Grand Rapids High School Marching Band.
  • Youth in Music – 10/14/2023 GR finished 4th for class AAA and are the Minnesota State Class AAA Championship Runners Up for the second year in a row. Plus, they earned 5th best band in the state overall.
