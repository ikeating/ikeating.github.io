Well, here we are with my last entry for January, 2024! It has been a bit of a journey this last month attempting to write something every day. I haven’t found my writing voice yet and I think that actually may be chasing a mirage, but I have found it to be a great brain exercise. I am definitely going to continue whether I have bad or good inspirational days.

Some observations from the last month; I like doing a week-recap on Sunday for journaling, I have been pushing my writing to GitHub everyday which keeps me fresh with that procedure, its ok to use a couple days to complete something containing more content, and I will have days when I miss and that is ok.

I would like to focus my writing towards a specific interest area rather than just picking ideas from the blue, I feel like if I narrow my topic, I can build and grow on that. Another idea, use off days to write a letter to someone…

Interesting note for today it currently is 44 degrees out! This warm weather is crazy. At 1456 hours it reached 50 degrees at the Grand Rapids airport.

End of blog. Thank you for reading!
