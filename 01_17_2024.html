01/17/2024 GitHub

Tonight, I moved this, my collections of writing, into my git repository for https://ikeating.github.io. I’m not quite sure what I’m doing with it yet. While I was doing this, I updated my introductory page with new employment information. I really don’t have any great ideas yet about how to share my writings to my page. It’s created with HTML and I have been writing within a Word document so I’d have to figure that out.

I think for now all I would want is a simple list of months. Each month would contain all of my writings for that month. I suppose I could simply cut and paste all of a months’ worth of writing and put it into a linked HTML page. Seems kind of crude though. I’m cringing at what my brother would think of this idea:)

I do like the idea of my writing getting backed up, and stored, within my GitHub account. I’m sure a better idea will come along, so for now I’ll just focus on the writing.

End of blog. Thank you for reading!
