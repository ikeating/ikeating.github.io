Flight Regimes

"This poster is a visual guide to the different types of flight that vehicles can achieve, from airplanes flying in the atmosphere to rockets traveling into deep space. It shows how altitude (how high you go) and velocity (how fast you go) determine what kind of flight is possible, whether it's a jet breaking the sound barrier, a satellite orbiting Earth, or a spacecraft leaving the solar system. By mapping out everything from subsonic airplanes to interstellar probes, it highlights the challenges like heating, gravity, and speed that engineers and scientists must overcome for different types of missions."
